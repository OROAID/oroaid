About OROAID

OROAID is a humanitarian organization committed to helping people affected by crisis, poverty, and displacement. We work with local communities to deliver urgent assistance while building long-term pathways to stability.

Mission

To provide compassionate, fast, and fair support to people in need—together in unity.

Values

Unity • Compassion • Integrity • Community leadership • Accountability
